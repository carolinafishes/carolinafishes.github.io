PhyInformR

This is a walkthrough of PhyInformR broken into three main sections

Part 1: Installation and Phylogenetic Informativeness Profiles

Go to Part 1

This section covers the basics of installation and how to get site rates using IQTREE2. Also featured is a demonstration of how to import this external data and the dependencies PhyInformR utilizes.

Once you have imported your site rate file and your guide tree this section covers how to generate PI profiles as well as a discussion of how to interpret these curves

Part 2: Quantification of Signal and Noise

Go to Part 2

This section focuses on advanced features and calculations of quartet resolution probabilities

This calculations derive from Townsend et al. (2012), who introduced theory that takes into the account the interplay of site rates, time, and internode length between species divergences to assess the predicted probabilty of data contributing to accurate topological resolution

Part 3: Advanced visualizations

Go to Part 3

This part focuses on visualization. The quantitative framework of quartet internode calculations lends itself wonderfully to the development of new ways to visualize information in a given dataset. This section highlights how to generate graphics from a recent publications

We anticipate this to be an area of rapid growth so please check back often for new ways to visualize these metrics. Also, if you develop a new way to view these data please let us know and we will gladly post the code and link(s) to your manuscript(s)
