TOAST

TOAST provides a suite of utility functions for sequence analysis and phylogenomics

TOAST was originally designed to aid in transcriptomic data processing, and continues to expand to work with any type of sequence data and provide general utility functions as well as functions that assist with phylogenomic dataset assembly, visualization of missing data patterns, and dataset filtration and reassembly
